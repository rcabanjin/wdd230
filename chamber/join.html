BCIMA Membership

No matter your level of marketing experience, the BCIMA offers the tools, training, research and community to help you advance your career.

Who Should Join the AMA?

  • Marketing and sales professionals of all levels looking to boost their skills and expand their networks
  • Small business owners, freelancers or career switchers looking to learn more about marketing
  • Scholars, academics and doctoral students looking to publish in a journal or collaborate on research
  • Students looking for more connection in the Marketing Industry

Membership level benefits:

  • Student Members can join one of our 320+ Collegiate Chapters around the world
  • Professional Members can be a part of one of our 70+ Professional Chapters across North America
  • Academic Members get unlimited access to Special Interest Groups (SIGs)
  • Gold Membership: Trainings, advertising, event discounts
