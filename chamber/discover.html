Vancouver City

The City

Get to know more about Vancouver and the surrounding Metro Vancouver region. From colourful downtown neighbourhoods, to culturally diverse suburbs and mountainside districts, there’s plenty to explore!

Geography

Metro Vancouver

Vancouver, a bustling west coast seaport in British Columbia, is among Canada’s densest, most ethnically diverse cities. A popular filming location, it’s surrounded by mountains, and also has thriving art, theatre and music scenes. Vancouver Art Gallery is known for its works by regional artists, while the Museum of Anthropology houses preeminent First Nations collections.

  • Population (2021): 662,248
  • Land Area: 115.18 km2
  • Time zone: UTC−08:00 (PST)
  • GDP (Vancouver CMA) $154.3 billion (2018)

Vancouver Activities

A cosmopolitan city right on the edge of nature, Vancouver makes it easy to combine an urban getaway with outdoor adventure. From family-friendly fun to thrill-seeking exploits; from marine exploring to mountaintop luxury, you'll find plenty of things to do in Vancouver.

Adventure

Vancouver is the perfect destination for those looking for the adventure of a lifetime! From heart-pounding thrills set against a spectacular backdrop, through to serene journeys into nature, and family-friendly fun, you'll find plenty of options to create smiles and build life-long memories.

Beaches

Which way to the beach? Well, in this city you can find one everywhere you turn. You might not associate Vancouver with beaches, but whether you're looking for a stretch of sand to spread out and relax with a good book, an outdoor court to spike a volleyball, or a catwalk to strut your stuff, you’ll find it here.
