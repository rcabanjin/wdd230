About us

The British Columbia Industrial Marketing Association (BCIMA) is a non-profit organization that brings together marketers from all industrial sectors to connect, learn, and be inspired. BCIMA has aims to provide industrial marketing professionals with the opportunities and resources to advance their careers and bring the latest updates and trends in B2B marketing. BCIMA intent is to established an association of like-minded marketers who all aim for a singular pursuit: success.
